## ‚≠ê 2. GAME ICON (THE MOST IMPORTANT ASSET)

Your icon must:

- **Be simple, strong, readable at 60x60**
- **Use 1 focal point** (cat face)
- **Use high contrast**
- **Glow**
- **Look soft and magical**
- **Convey "cosmic + cute" in <0.3 sec**

### 2.1 Suggested Icon Styles

**Option A: Celestial Cat Face** ‚≠ê **RECOMMENDED FOR LAUNCH**

**Design:**
- Cat face centered
- Bright star eyes
- Nebula around head
- Soft purple glow circle
- High contrast
- Simple, strong

**Why:** Most readable, instantly recognizable, cute + cosmic

**Option B: Chibi Nebula Cat**

**Design:**
- Tiny big-eyed cartoon version
- Star markings on cheeks
- Subtle galaxy effect behind
- Cute, approachable
- Soft colors

**Why:** Very cute, appeals to younger audience, friendly

**Option C: Prestige Cat Icon**

**Design:**
- Gold outline
- Void flames
- Cat profile silhouette
- Mysterious, premium
- Dark background

**Why:** Premium feel, mysterious, appeals to older players

### 2.2 Icon Design Specifications

**Technical Requirements:**
- **Size:** 512x512 (Roblox requirement)
- **Format:** PNG with transparency
- **Readable at:** 60x60 minimum
- **Background:** Transparent or solid dark
- **Style:** Soft, glowing, cosmic
- **Colors:** Purple, blue, gold, white

**Design Principles:**
- **One focal point** - Cat face/head
- **High contrast** - Stands out on any background
- **Glow effect** - Soft, magical
- **Simple shapes** - Readable at small sizes
- **Brand colors** - Purple, blue, gold
