## üé≠ 2. CORE ANIMATION PRINCIPLES

### 2.1 Motion Principles

**Easing:**
- **Ease In/Out** - Smooth acceleration and deceleration
- **No Linear** - Avoid constant speed
- **Gentle Curves** - Soft, organic motion paths
- **Natural Physics** - Slight anticipation and follow-through

**Timing:**
- **Slow and Expressive** - Deliberate, meaningful movements
- **Not Rushed** - Give animations time to breathe
- **Varied Pacing** - Different speeds for different actions
- **Weightless Feel** - Slight floaty quality

**Spacing:**
- **Smooth Arcs** - Natural motion paths
- **No Straight Lines** - Organic, curved movements
- **Anticipation** - Small wind-up before action
- **Follow-Through** - Gentle settle after action

### 2.2 Cat-Like Characteristics

**Authentic Feline Behavior:**
- **Tail Expressiveness** - Tail conveys mood and emotion
- **Ear Movement** - Ears react to environment and mood
- **Body Language** - Posture shows personality
- **Graceful Movement** - Smooth, controlled motion
- **Playful Elements** - Cute, endearing behaviors

**Stylized Elements:**
- **Slightly Exaggerated** - More expressive than real cats
- **Cosmic Twist** - Weightless, floaty quality
- **Elegant Poses** - Refined, beautiful positions
- **Magical Feel** - Ethereal, otherworldly

### 2.3 Cosmic Weightlessness

**Floaty Elements:**
- **Slight Hover** - Subtle upward float
- **Slow Descent** - Gentle landing
- **Weightless Jumps** - Extended air time
- **Soft Landing** - Gentle settle
- **Floating Accessories** - Ornaments drift slightly

**Implementation:**
- Add slight vertical offset in idle
- Extend jump/fall timing
- Soften landing impact
- Gentle bounce on landing
- Subtle drift in movement
